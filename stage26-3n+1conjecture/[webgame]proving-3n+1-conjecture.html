Collatz正奇数回归树生成规则(Collatz-Odd-Tree Generation Rule)

  1. v(x)= (x%3==2)? 2*x-1 :(x%3==1? 4*x-1:null)); to generate the first subnode of x
  2. h(x)=4*x+1 to generate the next sibling of x
  3. x is a leaf node in the full Collatz-Odd-Tree iff (x%3==0)
